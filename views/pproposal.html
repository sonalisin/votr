Project Proposal

Our project proposal is to address the issue of accessibility to voting. Traditionally when speaking about accessibility you would think of those with disabilities or physical barriers preventing them from voting. While this will be a focus of our work we've decided to broaden our approach and also look at social barriers preventing people, predominantly youth from engaging with the political system and voting.

By broadening the definition in this way we will be looking at creating a platform to engage people from diverse social backgrounds in the political debate, creating fun, engaging content to explain our political and voting systems as well provide an easy to understand, appealing explanation of key political parties and their views as well interactive content.
